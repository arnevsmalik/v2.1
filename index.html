Dive into hands-on learning, real moot court battles, and mentorship from the best. This isn't just theory—it's your launchpad into the world of law.

Day 5: Celebrate & Connect
Score feedback, win awards, and join our exclusive alumni network for ongoing mentorship.

Why Article 0?

Legal education in India needs a shakeup. We're here to make it real, practical, and accessible for everyone.
  • Real-World Skills: We bridge the gap between what you learn in class and what you need in court.
  • Grow Your Confidence: Research, write, argue, and win—supported by mentors who care.
  • Find Your People: Connect with justice-minded students and build friendships that last.
  • Open to All: No matter where you're from, you belong here. We're breaking barriers, not building them.
